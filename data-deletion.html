Data Deletion Request Policy

Effective date: 5 June 2026

This page explains how users can request deletion of data associated with Matchday Football.

Developer: Rajkumar Sarangajit

Support: sarangajitrk@gmail.com

Data Deletion URL: https://sarangajitrk.github.io/matchday-football-legal/data-deletion.html

1. No Account Required

The launch version of the app does not require users to create an account. Because there is no account system at launch, there is no account deletion flow.

However, the app may still store limited server-side data needed for app functionality, such as app instance IDs, push notification tokens, notification preferences, favorite team selections if synced, support requests, and crash/error diagnostics.

2. How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of data associated with your app installation or support request, email:

sarangajitrk@gmail.com

Subject suggestion:

Data Deletion Request - Matchday Football

Please include your in-app Support ID or app instance ID if available. Do not send sensitive personal information.

3. What We Can Delete

Depending on what data exists, we may delete or deactivate:

  • Push notification tokens
  • Server-side notification preferences
  • Favorite team preferences stored server-side
  • App instance records
  • Support messages associated with your email, where applicable
  • Crash/error records where reasonably identifiable and deletable

4. Local Device Data

Some data may be stored only on your device, such as local preferences or cached app data. We cannot delete purely local device data remotely.

You can delete local app data by clearing the app's storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

5. Retention and Exceptions

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days.

Some information may be retained where necessary for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, debugging, or audit purposes.

Retention schedule:

  • Deletion requests: processed within 30 days
  • Crash/error logs: up to 12 months
  • Support emails: up to 2 years
  • Sports/debug logs: up to 90 days unless needed longer for security or debugging
  • Push tokens/preferences: until disabled, invalid, inactive, or deletion request
