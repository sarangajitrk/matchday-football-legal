Privacy Policy

Effective date: 5 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Matchday Football ("the app", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the app.

Developer: Rajkumar Sarangajit

Support: sarangajitrk@gmail.com

Package name: com.sarangajitrk.matchdayfootball

Privacy Policy URL: https://sarangajitrk.github.io/matchday-football-legal/privacy.html

1. Summary

Matchday Football is an accountless football companion app that provides live football scores, match stories, match context, standings, notifications, and shareable match cards.

At launch:

  • The app is free to use.
  • The app does not use advertising SDKs.
  • The app does not require users to create an account.
  • The app does not use analytics SDKs.
  • The app does not use AI processing.
  • The app does not sell personal information.

We collect and use only the information needed to provide app functionality, deliver selected notifications, maintain app security, and diagnose app crashes or errors.

2. Information We Collect

The app may collect or process the following information:

2.1 App preferences and settings

  • Favorite team selections
  • Notification preferences
  • Theme preference
  • App language/region/timezone settings where needed for app functionality

2.2 Push notification information

  • Push notification token
  • App instance identifier
  • Platform and delivery information needed to send notifications

2.3 App instance and device-related identifiers

  • A pseudonymous app instance ID
  • Basic app version, platform, and device diagnostic information needed for security, notification delivery, and crash/error reporting

2.4 Crash and error information

If Sentry is enabled in the production app, the app may collect crash reports, error logs, stack traces, app version, device/platform details, and related diagnostic information. Crash reporting should be configured to avoid collecting unnecessary personal information where available.

2.5 Support information

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message.

2.6 Sports data

Sports fixtures, live scores, match events, standings, and related tournament information are provided by third-party sports data providers such as SportsMonks. We do not send your personal identifiers, push tokens, preferences, or support messages to SportsMonks.

3. Information We Do Not Collect at Launch

At launch, the app does not collect:

  • Name, phone number, postal address, or account username
  • Precise GPS location or approximate location
  • Contacts
  • Photos, videos, or audio files
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Calendar data
  • Messages or SMS
  • User-generated public content
  • Payment card details
  • Advertising ID for advertising purposes

Payment card details are not collected by us. If paid features are introduced in a future update, payments will be handled by Google Play or another disclosed billing provider, and the Privacy Policy and Play Store disclosures will be updated before those features are made available.

4. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • Provide live scores, fixtures, match details, standings, and match stories
  • Store or apply favorite team selections and app preferences
  • Send match notifications you choose to receive
  • Diagnose crashes, errors, and app stability issues
  • Prevent abuse, protect app security, and maintain service reliability
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Push Notifications

Push notifications are optional.

If you grant notification permission, we process your push notification token to deliver match alerts and app notifications selected by you. Push notification tokens are processed based on your consent, expressed through granting notification permission on your device. You may withdraw consent at any time through your device settings or app notification settings.

Notification delivery may rely on Capacitor Push Notifications and Firebase Cloud Messaging. Notifications may be delayed, missed, duplicated, or unavailable due to device settings, operating system behavior, network conditions, service provider availability, or app errors.

6. Third-Party Services

At launch, the app may use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: backend storage and app functionality. Data may be processed and stored in the region selected for our Supabase project and may also be processed by service providers in other countries where necessary to provide the app, maintain security, troubleshoot issues, and comply with legal obligations.
  • SportsMonks API: football fixtures, scores, match events, standings, and related sports data, used server-side
  • Capacitor Push Notifications and Firebase Cloud Messaging: push notification registration and delivery
  • Sentry: crash/error reporting and diagnostic logs
  • GitHub Pages: hosting legal pages

The app does not use advertising SDKs at launch. The app does not use analytics SDKs at launch.

If we introduce new services or features that materially change how we collect, use, or share information, we will update this Privacy Policy, update related Play Store disclosures where required, and provide additional notice where appropriate before those changes apply.

7. Legal Bases for Users in the EEA/UK

Where GDPR or similar laws apply, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Push notifications: consent
  • Favorite teams, notification preferences, theme preferences, and core app functionality: performance of the service requested by you
  • Crash/error logs and app security: legitimate interests in maintaining a secure and reliable app
  • Support messages: your request and our legitimate interests in responding to support
  • Compliance records: legal obligations where applicable

You may withdraw notification consent at any time through your device settings or app notification settings.

8. Data Sharing

We share information only as needed to operate the app and provide services.

Examples:

  • Supabase may process app data used for backend functionality.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging may process push tokens and notification delivery data.
  • Sentry may process crash/error diagnostics.
  • SportsMonks provides sports data to our backend; we do not send user identifiers or personal preferences to SportsMonks.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use advertising SDKs at launch.

9. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described above.

Retention schedule:

  • Favorite teams, theme, and notification preferences: until removed by the user, app reset, app inactivity cleanup, or deletion request
  • Push tokens: until notification permission is withdrawn, the token becomes invalid, the app is inactive for a reasonable period, or deletion is requested
  • Crash/error logs: up to 12 months
  • Support emails: up to 2 years
  • Sports/debug logs: up to 90 days, unless needed for security, debugging, dispute resolution, or legal compliance
  • Data deletion requests: processed within 30 days, subject to information we must retain for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or compliance reasons

11. California Privacy Notice

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

If you are a California resident, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information associated with your app installation or support communications, subject to applicable exceptions. You may contact us at sarangajitrk@gmail.com.

Because Matchday Football does not use advertising SDKs at launch, we do not provide a separate “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information” link in the app. If this changes in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and related disclosures before enabling such features.

12. Children and Minimum Age

The app is intended for users aged 13 and older. It is not directed to children under 13 or under the applicable minimum digital age in their jurisdiction.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below the applicable minimum age. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can delete it where required.

13. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. Data transmitted between the app, our backend, and third-party services should use HTTPS/TLS or equivalent secure transport.

No system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users should keep their devices and operating systems updated.

14. International Processing

The app and service providers may process information in countries other than your country of residence. Our backend is hosted using Supabase, and data may be processed and stored in the region selected for the Supabase project. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards or service-provider commitments for cross-border processing.
